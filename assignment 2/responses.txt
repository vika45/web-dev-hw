1. Data travels from one place to another by lots and lots of 1s and 0s being delivered by electronic pulses, light beams, and radio waves.
2. Internet Protocol Address is where information from other devices send information, this IPS is a series of numbers that lead us to where we want to go. The Domain Name System (dns) converts these numbers in to a name, such as google.com.
3. A cookie is invisible Data that a browsers sees and knows to save. The data that is saved is essentially an ID card for that given website that helps to identify who you are.
