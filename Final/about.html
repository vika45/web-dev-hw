Who are we?

We are a small, locally owned and operated buisness! We have been open since 1987, when our grandfather opened up the store himself. While times and travel have changed, our core values remain! We are dedicated to being as helpful to you as we possibly can, we offer all different views of the island that regular tourists might miss!
